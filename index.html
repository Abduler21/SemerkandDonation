Information

Two devastating earthquakes have struck Turkey, leaving behind widespread destruction. So far, 16,500 lives have perished, 100,000 injured and the number is estimated to rise as more people are being found in the rubble. Join the Aid Effort for the Turkish Earthquake Victims - Donate Today! The affected communities desperately need our support. Your donation can make a difference in the lives of those affected by providing essential aid such as food, shelter, and medical care. Every donation, no matter how small, can help bring hope and comfort to those in need. Together, we can help the people of Turkey rebuild their lives and communities. Donate now to show your support and help make a difference in the lives of those affected by the earthquake. Thank you for your generosity.
